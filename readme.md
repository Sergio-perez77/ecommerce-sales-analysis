# E-commerce Sales & Revenue Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analizes e-commerce transactional data to identify key business insights related to revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends.

##Tools Used
-Exel.
-SQL.
-Tableau.
-Kaggle Dataset.
-Linux Terminal.

##Key KPIs
- Total Revenue: **9.75M**
- Top Market: **United Kingdom (8.19M)**
- Best-selling Product: **World War 2  Glidders Assorted Designs**
- Top Customer: **ID 14646**
- Peak Sales Period: **Q4 / Holiday Season**

##Key Insights
- Revenue is highly concentrated in te UK market.
- A small group of customers drives a large share of revenue.
- Product sales are concentrated among a few top SKUs.
- Sales peaks occur mainly in november and december.
